## Part B — One-page talking points

*A single-page reference to put under the videos or hand out in a room.*

**One-liner**
A "newsroom in your pocket" — AI reporters and an editor do the legwork; a human
always writes the final word.

**What it is**
A small, low-cost service that watches the topics you choose, curates only what's
significant (with sources), and delivers Morning/Afternoon/Final editions plus a live
dashboard — assembled by a human, never auto-published.

**How it works (30 seconds)**
- **Reporters** — one focused AI agent per topic ("beat"); add a beat in minutes.
- **Editor** — deterministic, no-AI step that de-dupes, drops old/low-value items,
  ranks official + high-impact first.
- **Human** — reviews and writes the final piece. Nothing ships unreviewed.

**Why it matters**
- Stay current without the daily scan; **only the significant stuff**, sourced.
- **Cheap by design** — small model for reporters, zero-cost editing.
- **Scales gently** — a new topic is one more small task, not a bigger bill.

**The values ("Human AND")**
- Human authorship and review — no autonomous publishing.
- Fair pay and credit for creators; consent and privacy respected.
- Editorial independence from advertising; everything sourced and auditable.

**Where it goes (use cases)**
- **Charity campaigns** — competitive intelligence across your cause area.
- **Real estate** — daily market research for agents, investors, developers.
- **Competitive / market intelligence** — track rivals and your sector.
- **Policy & regulatory watch** — GRC, compliance, government action.
- **Local & community storytelling** — accessible, translated, locally authored.

**Proof (live today)**
Two beats already running — an AI export-control story and a PwC-Canada
competitor watch — with a working dashboard and independently verified sources.

**The ask**
Let's pilot it on two topics for one week and see what the briefings feel like.
